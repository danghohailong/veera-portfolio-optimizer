    Quant Optimizer Pipeline

    Log returns, annualized covariance, MPT Monte Carlo, and Markowitz frontier extraction.

    Step 2 — MPT Monte Carlo Weight Simulation

    Random valid weight vectors are sampled by rejection sampling under the IPS max-weight constraint.

    Step 3 — Markowitz Efficient Frontier

    The frontier is extracted from volatility buckets, keeping the best return in each bucket.
